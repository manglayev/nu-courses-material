How to quickly master novel skills

Sometimes, you may not have enough time dedicated in your schedule to learning novel skills. You might be super interested in learning and mastering skills but lack the time. This means you want to learn these skills quickly. Read on to learn some valuable tips on how to master the novel skills quickly.

  1. Establish clear goals
  2. Separate skills into sub-skills
  3. Identify challenges
  4. Use the Pareto rule
  5. Focus on a single skill
  6. Learn in a group
  7. Find a role model
  8. Utilise good sleep practices
  9. Find motivation
